ABOUT ME

I design products that reduce friction for users and move the dial for businesses by turning fuzzy problems into simple shippable solutions. With 20+ years experience across design & development, I lead the Product Development Lifecycle - from discovery to delivery - using research, UX/UI, design systems, and rapid prototyping using generative AI to help ship faster and better.

MY PROCESS

Design is about creativity and less about following formulae. Every single project, team, and challenge is varies from one to the other - and this calls for being flexible and pragmatic. Working in fast-paced (and dare I say oftentimes chaotic) environments has taught me to balance speed with craft, and adapt my approach accordingly. My process is to champion the user while collaborating closely with dev and being adaptive. Structure is what I rely on to stay focused, but knowing when to bend it is an indispensable skill - because in the end it is about solving the right problems and creating real value.
